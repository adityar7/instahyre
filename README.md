# Instahyre

Instahyre is where the top 2% of talent and companies meet each other. We make recruitment faster and better!

Traditional job boards have too much noise. As a job seeker, do you really want to look at thousands of completely irrelevant job postings, or get spammed by recruitment agencies for the same old jobs? Wouldn't you rather showcase yourself to a curated list of the top companies and let them reach out to you directly? That's exactly what Instahyre lets you do. Discover great jobs that are right just for you.

As an employer, you get exclusive access to the most premium talent available in the market. Our data-science based algorithms filter the candidates automatically based on your job openings, so you get a highly relevant set of candidates who are actively exploring new opportunities. All this, at a fraction of the price charged by recruitment agencies!

Instahyre is used by the top tech companies in India including the likes of Amazon, Flipkart, Uber, Ola, Grofers and Practo.
